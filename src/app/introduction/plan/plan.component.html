Before you start defining your new OKR, you have to plan two things

  1. Introduction of OKR + first iteration
  2. Default iteration
For each meeting/workshop you have to decide about
  • What is the goal
  • Who should attend
  • Date
  • Who is moderating
Each meeting/workshop needs a defined goal. This way everybody gets a clear understanding about the meaning of the meeting. Additionaly, I helps with setting the right expectations. While having the goal in mind, the list of necessary attendees will be drafted. Building up on this, the search for a date for all those attendees starts. Don't underestimate this step. It can easily throw off your whole schedule. Speaking of a schedule: You have to make a schedule including all your OKR-meetings and OKR-workshops. Without it, most likely you will face several delays.
